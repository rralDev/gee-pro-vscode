# Privacy Policy for GEE Pro VS Code Extension

**Last Updated: May 3, 2026**

GEE Pro ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our VS Code extension.

## 1. Data Collection
The Extension **does not collect, store, or share any personal data**. 
- We do not have access to your Google password.
- We do not store your Earth Engine scripts on our servers.
- All authentication tokens are stored locally on your machine using VS Code's secure `SecretStorage`.

## 2. Use of Google Data
The Extension uses Google OAuth2 to access the Google Earth Engine API on your behalf. This access is used strictly to:
- Execute Earth Engine scripts.
- Display geospatial data on the integrated map.
- Manage your Earth Engine assets (list, move, delete).

## 3. Third-Party Services
The Extension communicates directly with Google APIs. No third-party services have access to your data through this extension.

## 4. Security
Your authentication tokens are stored locally and encrypted by your operating system's keychain via VS Code. 

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues.
